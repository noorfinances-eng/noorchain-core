# 1. Purpose of this Document

This document defines the official guidelines and requirements for opening the **NOORCHAIN Foundation Bank Account** in Switzerland.

The account is mandatory for:

- receiving donations  
- receiving grants  
- receiving public or institutional funding  
- covering operational costs  
- maintaining transparent accounting  
- aligning with Swiss Legal Light CH  
- preparing Phase 6 (Genesis Pack) and Phase 7 (Mainnet)

This document does **NOT** create the foundation itself; it prepares the banking step **once the Foundation is officially incorporated**.

---

# 2. Legal Context (Switzerland)

### The bank account must belong to:
**NOORCHAIN Foundation (Switzerland)**  
A non-profit foundation under Swiss Civil Code Art. 60–79.

### Requirements:
- The Foundation must first be formally created  
- Statutes must be validated and signed  
- The Foundation must be registered at the Swiss Commercial Registry (Registre du Commerce)  
- A valid Swiss address is required  
- A President & Treasurer must be designated  
- Transparency requirements must be met  

This matches the legal framework defined in:

- Foundation Statutes (B1)  
- Governance Charter (C2)  
- Compliance Framework (D3)  
- Legal Architecture (E)

# 4. Required Documents

To open the account, the Foundation must provide:

### 4.1 Statutes  
Signed and dated **Foundation_Statutes_1.1.md**.

### 4.2 Founder Identification  
ID of founder(s) and board members (passport or ID card).

### 4.3 Proof of Swiss Address  
Address of the Foundation’s headquarters.

### 4.4 Minutes / Resolution  
A Foundation Board resolution stating:

- approval of account opening  
- authorization of signatories  
- account type (operational account)  

### 4.5 AML / KYC Form  
Banks will request:

- purpose of the foundation  
- expected cash flows  
- non-profit mission  
- confirmation of Legal Light CH  
- confirmation: **NO custody, NO PSP, NO fiat conversion**

# 6. Financial Compliance

### The bank account MUST adhere to:

- Swiss KYC (Know Your Customer)  
- AMLA (Anti-Money Laundering Act)  
- No speculative operations  
- No custody  
- No financial intermediation  

### Confirmations required by the Foundation:

✔ NOORCHAIN is **not a PSP**  
✔ NOORCHAIN cannot convert fiat ↔ crypto internally  
✔ NOORCHAIN does not promise financial returns  
✔ NUR is a **utility token** under Legal Light  
✔ PoSS is **non-financial**  

Banks generally accept blockchain foundations under these restrictions.

---

# 7. Account Structure

Recommended structure:

### 7.1 Main Account  
For operational payments and donations.

### 7.2 Optional Sub-Accounts  
Only if needed:

- Community grant account  
- Infrastructure account  
- Multi-sig associated crypto wallets (off-chain reference only)

Crypto wallets remain **separate** and are not bank custody.
