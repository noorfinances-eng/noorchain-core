NOORCHAIN — Pilot School Charter
Educational Pilot Program (Pre-Mainnet Phase 8.A)
Version 1.1
Last updated: 2025-XX-XX
1. Purpose of This Charter

This document defines the framework of an educational pilot program between NOORCHAIN and the pilot school École Graine de Vie (Switzerland).

The purpose of this pilot is to:

experiment with non-financial Proof of Signal Social (PoSS) mechanisms,

explore educational, social, and civic use cases of blockchain technology,

test ethical digital certification concepts in a controlled, pre-mainnet environment.

This pilot is experimental, non-commercial, and non-financial.

2. Scope of the Pilot

The pilot program is limited to:

educational demonstrations,

simulated or symbolic PoSS signals,

internal learning activities (students, educators, community),

testnet or off-chain representations where applicable.

The pilot does not involve:

real-money transactions,

financial incentives,

token speculation,

custody of assets,

public market exposure.

3. Nature of PoSS Signals (Educational Context)

Within this pilot, PoSS signals may represent:

participation in educational activities,

contribution to community or ecological initiatives,

validated learning milestones,

ethical or social actions aligned with the school’s values.

All signals are:

symbolic in nature,

non-financial,

non-transferable outside the pilot context,

validated manually or semi-manually.

4. Roles & Responsibilities
4.1 École Graine de Vie

Participates voluntarily in the pilot.

Defines educational activities and values.

Does not handle tokens, wallets, or technical infrastructure.

Acts as an educational partner, not as a validator or financial actor.

4.2 NOORCHAIN

Provides technical guidance and conceptual tools.

Ensures Legal Light CH compliance.

Does not collect personal data.

Does not promote financial usage.

Retains full responsibility for the protocol and infrastructure.

5. Legal & Compliance Framework

This pilot operates under the Swiss Legal Light CH framework:

No custody of user assets

No promised returns

No financial product

No investment solicitation

No fiat conversion

No regulated payment activity

NOORCHAIN remains a utility-focused, non-profit-oriented protocol during this phase.

6. Data Protection & Privacy

No personal student data is recorded on-chain.

No private identifiers are stored.

Any educational data remains internal to the school.

The pilot complies with Swiss and EU data protection principles.

7. Duration & Termination

This pilot is temporary and exploratory.

Either party may terminate participation at any time.

Termination has no financial, legal, or operational consequence.

8. Non-Exclusivity & Independence

This charter:

does not create exclusivity,

does not imply endorsement,

does not establish a legal partnership or joint venture,

does not create financial obligations.

9. Public Communication

Any public reference to this pilot:

must remain factual and non-promotional,

must avoid investment or financial language,

may highlight educational and social innovation only.

10. Acknowledgement

By participating in this pilot, both parties acknowledge:

the experimental nature of the initiative,

the educational purpose,

the absence of financial intent.
