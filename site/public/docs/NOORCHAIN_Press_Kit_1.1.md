1. Overview
What is NOORCHAIN?

NOORCHAIN is a Swiss, non-financial blockchain protocol designed to recognise positive human contribution through transparent, ethical, and verifiable digital signals.

It introduces PoSS — Proof of Social Signal, an innovative mechanism that validates real social actions such as:

participation in education

volunteer work

community involvement

cultural engagement

NOORCHAIN positions itself as social infrastructure, not a financial product.

It is built for:

NGOs

Schools & universities

Municipalities

Cultural institutions

Community programs

2. Official Description (Short)

NOORCHAIN is a Swiss ethical blockchain that recognises and validates positive human actions through a transparent, non-financial mechanism called PoSS (Proof of Social Signal).

It is designed as a trusted public-good infrastructure for institutions.

3. Key Messages (for media & partners)

NOORCHAIN is non-financial and non-custodial.

Built entirely within Swiss Legal Light CH guidelines.

Zero yield, zero staking, zero investment mechanics.

Purpose: recognise social contribution, not capital.

Fixed supply: 299,792,458 NUR (immutable).

Immutable PoSS reward split: 70% participant / 30% curator.

Halving cycle every 8 years, multi-decade horizon.

Curators are NGOs, schools, cultural and social institutions.

Technology: Cosmos SDK + Ethermint (EVM compatible).

Focus: education, NGOs, community programs, culture.

Vision: create a universal infrastructure for social contribution.
