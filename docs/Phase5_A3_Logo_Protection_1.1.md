# NOORCHAIN — Phase 5  
## A3 — Logo Protection (Trademark Logo Registration)  
### Version 1.1  
### Last Updated: 2025-12-03  

---

# 1. Purpose of this Document

This document defines the procedure for protecting the **NOORCHAIN visual identity**, specifically the logo, as part of Phase 5.

The goal is to ensure:

- legal protection of the visual brand  
- prevention of unauthorized or fraudulent use  
- alignment with the trademark of the word “NOORCHAIN”  
- future compatibility with exchanges and partners  
- long-term brand integrity for the Foundation  

This step is optional but strongly recommended before Phase 6 and Phase 7.

---

# 2. Type of Protection

The protection applies to:

### **1. The NOORCHAIN logo (vector format)**  
The logo is protected as a **figurative mark**.

### **2. Accepted formats**  
The applicant must prepare:

- SVG (preferred)  
- PNG (high resolution)  
- Black/white version  
- Monochrome version  

These formats ensure compatibility with IPI / EUIPO publication standards.

# 5. Filing Fees

## Switzerland (IGE/IPI)
Approximate fees:

- CHF 550 base fee  
- + CHF 100 per additional class  
→ Total ~ CHF 750

## EUIPO (optional)
- EUR 850 + 50 + 150  
→ Total ~ EUR 1,050

A figurative mark does not cost more than a word mark.

---

# 6. Procedure — Switzerland (IGE/IPI)

### Step 1 — Prepare Logo Files  
Must include:

- SVG  
- High-resolution PNG  
- Black & white variant  

### Step 2 — Online Filing  
On the IPI website:

- Applicant: NOORCHAIN Foundation (or “NOORCHAIN Foundation — In Formation”)  
- Type: Figurative Mark  
- Classes: 9, 36, 42  
- Upload logo file  

### Step 3 — Pay Fee  
Approx **CHF 750**.

### Step 4 — Examination (2–4 months)  
Check for conflicts.  
Check visual distinctiveness.

### Step 5 — Publication  
Logo appears in the Swiss Trademark Gazette.

### Step 6 — Protection Granted  
Duration: **10 years**, renewable indefinitely.
