1. Name, Form, and Headquarters

Name: NOORCHAIN Foundation

Form: Non-profit Association (Swiss Civil Code, Art. 60–79)

Headquarters: Switzerland (canton to be defined)

Legal personality upon adoption of statutes

2. Purpose of the Foundation

C’est la partie la plus importante :

Support development, maintenance and integrity of NOORCHAIN

Promote Proof of Signal Social (PoSS)

Ensure transparency, public good and Legal Light CH compliance

Publish governance rules

Manage the 5% Foundation allocation

Fund audits, documentation, partnerships, research

Provide neutral multi-sig oversight

No custody of user funds

No promise of returns

3. Activities

Funding open-source development

Coordination with NGOs, schools, curators

Publication of reports

Security audits

Community governance processes (non-binding)

Operating communication channels

4. Independence & Neutrality

No political or religious affiliation

No speculative behavior

No market manipulation

Transparency and neutrality required

5. Membership

Minimum 3 members

Admission and resignation rules

Membership register

Roles defined (Founder, Technical Member, Social Representative, Observer…)

6. Governing Bodies
6.1 General Assembly (GA)

Supreme organ

One meeting per year

Approval of financial reports

Amendments to statutes

Voting quorum and majorities

6.2 Foundation Board

3 to 5 members

Oversees long-term direction

Appoints representatives

Ensures Legal Light compliance

6.3 Multi-sig Committee (3/5)

On-chain representation of the Foundation

Signers:

Founder

NGO/Curator Representative

Technical Expert

Legal/Compliance Observer

Community or Educational Representative

Rotation rules

Transparency obligations

7. On-Chain Governance

The Foundation enforces on-chain rules but does not control users

No direct custody

No token sales

Changes to PoSS parameters via governance proposal

Genesis allocation responsibilities

8. Financial Resources

Membership fees (optional)

Donations

Grants

Foundation allocation (5% of Genesis)

Transparency report mandatory

No distribution of profits

9. Liability

No liability of members for debts

Foundation assets are solely responsible

Exclusion of responsibility for smart-contract risks

10. Transparency & Reporting

Annual activity report

Annual financial report

Publication of governance and PoSS parameter changes

11. Dissolution

Decision by General Assembly only

Assets must go to another Swiss non-profit aligned with public good

On-chain assets transferred to a burn address or public reserve

12. Signature & Adoption

Document adoption date

Signatures of founding members
