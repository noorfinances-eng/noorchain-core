Legal Architecture Overview

The legal governance structure of NOORCHAIN is composed of five independent layers:

1. NOORCHAIN Foundation (Non-Profit Association, Switzerland)

Role:

Holds mission and long-term vision

Ensures Legal Light CH compliance

Oversees governance and documentation

Manages the institutional 5% allocation

Supervises Curators and PoSS ecosystem

Characteristics:

Independent, non-custodial, non-investment

Transparent and mission-driven

Executes decisions through the Multi-sig Committee

2. Multi-sig Committee (3/5)

Role:

Executes approved governance decisions

Manages Foundation on-chain actions

Controls the institutional address

Applies PoSS parameter changes when required

Restrictions:

Cannot hold user assets

Cannot act without recorded governance decision

3. Curators (NGOs, schools, social organisations)

Role:

Validate PoSS signals (social contributions)

Maintain integrity of the reward process

Support social and educational initiatives

Rights and Restrictions:

Earn 30% of PoSS reward per signal

No governance authority

Must comply with ethical and transparency standards

4. Noor Dev Sàrl (Technical Development Entity)

Role:

Builds and maintains ecosystem tools

Conducts R&D and integrations

Holds the 5% development allocation on-chain

Restrictions:

No governance authority

No custody of user assets

Cannot operate financial services

5. Users (Self-Custodial Participants)

Role:

Interact with the blockchain

Emit PoSS signals

Maintain full control over their assets

Rights:

Full autonomy and self-custody

Can participate without intermediary

No KYC required for protocol access
