# 5. Registration Procedure — Switzerland (IGE/IPI)

### Step 1 — Search for identical names  
The name "NOORCHAIN" is sufficiently distinctive.

### Step 2 — Online Filing  
Submit through the IPI portal:

- Applicant: NOORCHAIN Foundation  
- Classes: 9, 36, 42  
- Mark Type: Word Mark  
- Mark: **NOORCHAIN**

### Step 3 — Pay Official Fees  
Swiss fees (approx):

- Base fee: CHF 550 (1 class)  
- + CHF 100 per additional class  
→ Total = approx **CHF 750**  

### Step 4 — Examination  
3 to 4 months.

### Step 5 — Publication  
Once accepted, the mark is published in the Swiss Trademark Gazette.

### Step 6 — Protection  
Valid for **10 years**, renewable indefinitely.

# 7. Governance Rules

### Ownership  
The trademark is owned exclusively by the Foundation.

### Use Authorization  
The Dev Sàrl receives a **non-exclusive, revocable license** to use the trademark for:

### Protection

- restrict abusive usage  
- deny unauthorized use  
- revoke usage rights if misused

- 3/5 multi-sig approval
